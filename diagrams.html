3. Activity Diagram (Core Flow)

This diagram maps out the step-by-step process of meal prediction, waste handling, and surplus donation.

stateDiagram-v2
    [*] --> ConfigureParameters
    ConfigureParameters: Admin configures future meal events
    
    ConfigureParameters --> AIAnalysis
    AIAnalysis: AI Engine analyzes historical attendance
    
    AIAnalysis --> GenerateForecast
    GenerateForecast: Forecast expected attendance & potential waste
    
    state drop_check <<choice>>
    GenerateForecast --> drop_check: Is attendance drop high?
    
    drop_check --> TriggerWarning: Yes (Significant Drop)
    drop_check --> NormalOperation: No
    
    TriggerWarning --> AdminDecision: Admin reviews warning
    
    state action_check <<choice>>
    AdminDecision --> action_check
    
    action_check --> AdjustPrep: Adjust food preparation amount
    action_check --> DonateSurplus: Donate surplus to NGO
    
    AdjustPrep --> [*]
    
    DonateSurplus --> NGOPickup
    NGOPickup: NGO portal tracks live pickup
    NGOPickup --> [*]
    
    NormalOperation --> [*]
